About the Show

Girls & Boys is a gripping, darkly humorous one-person show by acclaimed playwright Dennis Kelly. A woman tells the story of how she met the man she would marry — a story of love, ambition, and the seductive power of everyday optimism. But beneath the surface of this seemingly ordinary life, something far more unsettling is taking shape.

Told with raw intimacy and wit, Girls & Boys is a devastating meditation on gender, violence, and the stories we tell ourselves about the people we love. This Onsite Theatre Collective production reimagines the play within a site-specific environment, bringing its urgent emotional landscape into direct contact with its audience in a way no traditional stage can offer.

Written by Dennis Kelly
Directed by Christopher Marshall
